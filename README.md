# Roam

Pets moving on your screen, making your desktop more lively and fun!

## Includes

-   Add pets (45+ pets and characters available to choose from)
-   Add your own custom pet
-   Remove pets
-   Drag pets on your screen
-   Click through pets
-   Auto start up
-   Auto update
-   Allow as many pet/character as you want on your screen without any performance issue
-   Pet above taskbar
